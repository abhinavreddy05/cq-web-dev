My Fav. Places

ft.IIT Kharagpur

Amigos

Amigos is a restaurant that has definitely made a mark with its flavorful food. The menu is diverse and there are options for vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. From starters to desserts, every dish on the menu is bursting with taste and flavor. The menu has a variety of options, and it is difficult to pick a favorite, but some dishes that stand out include the steak fajitas, fish tacos, and shrimp ceviche.

Main Building

The main building of IIT Kharagpur is an iconic structure that is easily recognizable and holds a special place in the hearts of the institute's students, faculty, and alumni. Located at the heart of the campus, the building is a blend of classic and contemporary architectural styles, reflecting the institute's ethos of blending tradition with innovation.

TSC

The Tata Sports Complex is a state-of-the-art sports facility located on the campus of IIT Kharagpur. It is a world-class sports complex that is equipped with modern amenities and facilities for a wide range of sports. It has hosted several inter-collegiate and inter-university cricket matches over the years and is a favorite among cricket enthusiasts on campus.
